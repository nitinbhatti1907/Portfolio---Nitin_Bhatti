Browser-based practice tool for both CELPIP Writing tasks with official time limits (27 and 26 minutes), a split-panel exam layout keeping the question always visible alongside a planner and response area, live word count, pause and resume timer, browser spellcheck, and full session persistence via localStorage — zero dependencies, no sign-up required.
